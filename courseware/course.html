System Requirements

  • Browser

    1. An up-to-date version of Chrome or Firefox is strongly recommended.
    2. Mobile browsers are not supported.

  • Internet Connectivity

    1. Download: 2+ Mbps recommended; minimum 1 Mbps.
    2. Upload (for full courses & proctoring): 0.5 Mbps upload speed.

  • Operating System

    1. PC: Windows XP or higher with latest updates installed (note: Internet Explorer is not supported).
    2. Mac: OS X 10.6 or higher with latest updates installed.
    3. Linux: Any recent distribution that has the supported browsers installed.

  • YouTube

    1. Ability to watch YouTube videos is required. If YouTube is blocked on your network, please contact your IT support or internet provider.
    2. If you do not use the html5 player, you will need Adobe Flash Player v11 or above.
